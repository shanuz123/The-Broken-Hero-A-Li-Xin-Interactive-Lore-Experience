Li Xin (李信)

The Broken Hero

Vanguard 锋芒

Before the rust and the bloodstains, Li Xin was the razor edge of the Qin empire. He wasn't just a general; he was Ying Zheng’s apex predator, a prodigy carved from sheer discipline and lethal ambition. When he drew his blade, it was the absolute word of law. He was the golden child of a rising dynasty, carrying the weight of a unified continent on shoulders that refused to bow. The battlefield was his personal canvas, and his sword painted nothing but absolute, flawless victory.

The Fall 陨落

But ambition is a blindfold, and pride is the executioner. He pushed too far, misjudged the shadows of his enemy, and led his loyal vanguard straight into a total meat grinder. The agonizing screams of tens of thousands of his own men became the only lullaby he would ever hear again. Death would have been a mercy, a warrior’s quiet exit. Instead, he got the ultimate, gut-wrenching curse: survival. He crawled out of the carnage with his honor shattered, choking on the ash of his own ruined legend.

Abyss 深渊

Desperation breeds monsters. Hollowed out by grief and sick with the stench of his own failure, he reached out into the dark—and the dark grabbed his hand back. He struck a brutal bargain with a forbidden, abyssal force just to feel his pulse again. The power flooded his veins like liquid obsidian, replacing his broken pride with raw, unfiltered malice. It wasn't a resurrection; it was a hostile takeover. He bought his strength back, but the currency he used was his own humanity.

Shadow Form 暗形态

Fueled by anger and regret. Prioritizes raw destruction over control. Will annihilate anything in its path.

🌑

Light Form 光形态

Represents his former self. Fights with control, purpose, and discipline. Seeks redemption.

🌞

Eternal 永恒

He is no longer a man, but a permanent scar on the map of the empire. Li Xin exists in the shivering space between a heartbeat and a death rattle, a ghost who refuses to haunt and a soldier who refuses to die. Whether he is draped in the blinding gold of a dead sun or the suffocating ink of the void, he remains the ultimate paradox: a hero forged in failure. He is the end of the story that never truly stops bleeding, a silhouette standing at the edge of the world, eternally waiting for a battle that is finally large enough to consume him.

"He fights not just enemies—but himself."
